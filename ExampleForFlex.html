My Favourite Books

Atomic Habits
A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones, by James Clear.
The Alchemist
A philosophical story about following your dreams, written by Paulo Coelho.
Rich Dad Poor Dad
A personal finance classic teaching the difference between assets and liabilities, by Robert T. Kiyosaki.
Sapiens
An exploration of human history and evolution by Yuval Noah Harari.
To Kill a Mockingbird
A novel about racial injustice and moral growth in the American South, by Harper Lee.
